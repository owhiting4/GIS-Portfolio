El Morro Viewshed Analysis

Line-of-sight visibility from historic cannon positions in Old San Juan, Puerto Rico

This map visualizes line-of-sight visibility from cannon positions along the walls of Castillo San Felipe del Morro in Old San Juan, Puerto Rico. Using high-resolution elevation data from NOAA, a geodesic viewshed analysis was performed to model what defenders could see from the fort as they monitored both ocean and coastal approaches.

The resulting map shows cumulative visibility, where darker areas indicate locations visible from multiple cannon positions and lighter areas indicate more limited visibility. These patterns highlight how El Morro’s elevated position and coastal orientation created strong overlapping fields of view while still leaving subtle gaps that may have influenced defensive strategy.
